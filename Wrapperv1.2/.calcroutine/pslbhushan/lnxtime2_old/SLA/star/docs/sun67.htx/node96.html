SLA_EPJ - MJD to Julian Epoch   

ACTION:
Convert Modified Julian Date to Julian Epoch.
CALL:
D = sla_EPJ (DATE)

GIVEN:

DATE D Modified Julian Date (JD-2400000.5)

RETURNED:

sla_EPJ D Julian Epoch

REFERENCE:
Lieske, J.H., 1979. Astr.Astrophys., 73, 282.
