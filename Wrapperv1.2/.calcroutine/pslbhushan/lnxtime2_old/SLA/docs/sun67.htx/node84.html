SLA_DVN - Normalize Vector   

ACTION:
Normalize a 3-vector, also giving the modulus (double precision).
CALL:
CALL sla_DVN (V, UV, VM)

GIVEN:

V D(3) vector

RETURNED:

UV D(3) unit vector in direction of V
VM D modulus of V

NOTE:
If the modulus of V is zero, UV is set to zero as well.
