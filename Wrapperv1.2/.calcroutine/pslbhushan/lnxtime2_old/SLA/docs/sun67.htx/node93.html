SLA_EPB - MJD to Besselian Epoch   

ACTION:
Conversion of Modified Julian Date to Besselian Epoch.
CALL:
D = sla_EPB (DATE)

GIVEN:

DATE D Modified Julian Date (JD-2400000.5)

RETURNED:

sla_EPB D Besselian Epoch

REFERENCE:
Lieske, J.H., 1979, Astr.Astrophys. 73, 282.
