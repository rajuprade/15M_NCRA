SLA_GEOC - Geodetic to Geocentric   

ACTION:
Convert geodetic position to geocentric.
CALL:
CALL sla_GEOC (P, H, R, Z)

GIVEN:

P D latitude (geodetic, radians)
H D height above reference spheroid (geodetic, metres)

RETURNED:

R D distance from Earth axis (AU)
Z D distance from plane of Earth equator (AU)

NOTES:
1.
Geocentric latitude can be obtained by evaluating ATAN2(Z,R).
2.
IAU 1976 constants are used.

REFERENCE:
Green, R.M., 1985. Spherical Astronomy, Cambridge U.P., p98.
